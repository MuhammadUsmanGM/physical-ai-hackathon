# Module 0: Introduction to Physical AI

## Focus and Theme: AI Systems in the Physical World. Embodied Intelligence.

This foundational module introduces the core concepts of Physical AI and embodied intelligence. Students will understand the fundamental differences between digital AI and AI systems that function in physical environments, comprehending the physical laws that govern robot behavior and interaction.

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Define Physical AI and embodied intelligence
- Understand the importance of Physical AI in modern robotics
- Identify key challenges and opportunities in embodied AI systems
- Recognize the relationship between intelligence and physical embodiment

### Module Structure

This module contains the following topics:
- Foundations of Physical AI
- The Embodiment Hypothesis
- Physical Reasoning and Understanding
- Applications of Physical AI
- Course Overview and Expectations

### Prerequisites

Students should have:
- Basic understanding of AI and machine learning concepts
- Fundamental knowledge of physics and mathematics
- Programming experience in Python or C++
